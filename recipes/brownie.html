Double chip brownies

This double chip brownies recipe will make delicious treat to share with anyone!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups hershey's cocoa
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2/3 cup multed butter
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup hersheys milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup reeses peanut butter chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease baking pan
  2. Stir cocoa and baking soda in bowl, stir in butter until smooth
  3. Add flour, vanilla, and salt until blended
  4. Stir in milk chocolate chips and peanut butter chips
  5. Spread in prepared pan
  6. Bake 35-40 minutes or until brownies begin to pull away from sides of pan
  7. Cool completely on wire rack
  8. Enjoy!
