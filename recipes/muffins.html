Chocoalte chip muffins

This chocolate chip muffin recipe makes the most soft and delicious muffin to enjoy any time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup melted and cooled unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1-1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and spray muffin tra with non-stick grease
  2. Stir flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips in large bowl
  3. In seperate bowl, mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, milk, and vanilla
  4. Slowly add mixture to the dry ingredients until just combined
  5. Divide batter into 12 muffin cups
  6. Bake at 425 degrees F for 5 minutes, then reduce oven to 375 degrees F for 12-15 minutes
  7. Let cool for 5-10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
